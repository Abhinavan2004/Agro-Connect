Pradhan Mantri Kisan MaanDhan Yojana (PM-KMY)

- Launch Date: 12th September 2019
- Nature: Central sector scheme
- Objective: Provide security to vulnerable farmer families
- Scheme Type: Contributory
- Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers (SMFs) meeting exclusion criteria
- Membership: Farmers can opt to join by paying monthly subscription to the Pension Fund
- Government Contribution: Central Government matches the amount contributed by farmers
- Implementation: Aimed at providing pension benefits to enrolled farmers, enhancing their social security.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

- Launch Year: 2016
- Purpose: Provide a simple and affordable crop insurance product to ensure comprehensive risk cover for crops to farmers against all non-preventable natural risks from pre-sowing to post-harvest and to provide adequate claim amount
- Scheme Type: Demand-driven
- Eligibility: Available for all farmers
- Impact: A total of 5549.40 lakh farmer applications were insured under the scheme since 2016-17 and Rs 150589.10 crore has been paid as claim.

Ayushman Sahakar Scheme

- Launch Year: 2023
- Purpose: The Ayushman Sahakar Scheme is a government-funded program that provides financial assistance to cooperative societies in India to help them improve their health infrastructure.
- Budget: Rs. 10,000 crore.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

- Launch Date: 24th February 2019
- Nature: Central sector scheme
- Purpose: PM-KISAN is a central sector scheme launched on 24th February 2019 to supplement financial needs of land holding farmers, subject to exclusions.
- Benefit: Under the scheme, financial benefit of Rs. 6000/- per year is transferred in three equal four-monthly installments into the bank accounts of farmers families across the country, through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode.
- Amount Transferred: Till now, Rs. 2.81 lakh crores have been transferred through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to more than 11 crores beneficiaries (Farmers) through various instalments.

Modified Interest Subvention Scheme (MISS)

- Purpose: The Interest Subvention Scheme (ISS) provides concessional short term agri-loans to farmers practicing crop husbandry and other allied activities like animal husbandry, dairying, and fisheries.
- Availability: ISS is available to farmers availing short term crop loans up to Rs. 3.00 lakh at an interest rate of 7% per annum for one year.
- Additional Subvention: An additional 3% subvention is given to farmers for prompt and timely repayment of loans, thus reducing the effective rate of interest to 4% per annum.
